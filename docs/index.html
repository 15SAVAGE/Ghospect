About bot

Welcome to the world of our Discord bot!

Our bot is designed to make your server brighter and interactions between members more engaging.

With mini-games that lift spirits and add excitement, a points system that rewards activity, and the ability to purchase and customize roles, the bot becomes an integral part of your community.

Here, everyone will find something for themselves: from fun competitions to personalizing their profile.

Before installing on the server!!!

Be sure to read the instructions in the 'Server Installation' section.

What is this bot?

This is a Discord bot created to make your server more active and engaging.

What features and games does the bot have?

~Mini-games for fun and excitement.

~A points system that rewards member activity.

~Automatic role assignment and the ability to purchase roles for personalization.

What is the purpose of this bot?

It helps create a dynamic and fun community where members can interact, compete, and express their individuality through roles and activity.

Why should you choose this bot?

It's not just a tool, but an entire ecosystem for entertainment and communication that makes your server unique and memorable.

What mini-games are available?

OceanStar offers games such as quizzes, economic simulators, and simple arcade games.

How does the bot improve the server?

It adds gamification elements, encourages activity, and helps members feel like part of a friendly and engaging community.

How to manage the server using the bot?

Administrators can use commands like /ban, /kick, /mute, and others to manage members.

How to configure the bot?

To configure the bot, go to the 'Add to Server' section.
